About Me

/emmaschaale
/squinja

My name is Emma Schaale, and I'm a web designer and developer rolled into one. With a background in art ranging from photography to pixel art, I like to focus on the User Experience, whether that be in the application of a website, graphic poster, or even a video game. I'm currently working on publishing a visual novel game at KoiDev Games called 'Artificia', a choose-your-own-adventure type text-based game that tests your morality- you even get to date robots!
